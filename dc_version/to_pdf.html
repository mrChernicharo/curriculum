Background, Soft-Skills & Previous Work Experience

For around 15 years I have worked almost exclusively as a musician and a teacher. My communication and leadership skills were strongly developed in those years as I had to work with drastically different kinds of people all the time. 


Being a musician who rarely worked alone, I learned that dealing with groups can be tricky if you don’t have your soft skills up and running. Besides that, empathy, resilience and cordiality are 3 things that in my opinion, one must never forget about at work.


My journey as a programmer started in 2018. Music fulfilled me completely during my twenties but as I’ve grown more mature I started feeling that it was time for a change.


This feeling of learning something different, doing something meaningful is what made me understand that I wanted to build things, and that was when I discovered programming.


Now it's been over three years since I coded my first “Hello World” program, it was true love! Some time later I enrolled a new graduation course in Software Development, and the more I learn, the more I feel excited about the things I know that I don't know: The new discoveries to come.
